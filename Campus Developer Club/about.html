Join US
The campus front-end developer community is a community focusing on front-end development, and college students can join it to exchange front-end technologies, share experience, and help each other. The community is committed to improving members' front-end skills and helping them better cope with academic and professional development. In this community, members can participate in various activities, such as technical sharing sessions, code review meetings, project cooperation, online course learning, etc., to learn and progress with other members. In addition, the community will regularly hold some competitions and activities, such as front-end skills competitions, front-end project demonstrations, etc., so that members can exercise their skills, show their achievements, and compete with other members through competitions and activities. Joining the campus front-end developer community, you will have the opportunity to meet like-minded partners who also love the front-end and progress and grow together.
